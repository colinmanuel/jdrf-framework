What to Expect

Team JDRF to Cure Diabetes is a partnership: Runners commit to a fundraising minimum, with packages starting at $2,000. In exchange, JDRF provides runners with support and coaching in the months leading up to as well as throughout the Race weekend, so that runners can spend the weekend running, exploring, and enjoying the experience of a lifetime!

Some of what may be included:

Training Tee

Upon completing your registration, you will receive a Team JDRF technical tee, so that you can prepare for your race and tell the world you are running to help find a cure for T1D!

Group Celebration

Get together with your fellow Disney Runners and celebrate your accomplishments on and off the course!

Race Singlet

All runners will receive a Team JDRF to Cure Diabetes singlet to wear on Race day.

T-Shirt and Finish Line Medal

Upon crossing the finish line, all runners will receive a finish line t-shirt as well as a medal.

Lodging

The JDRF host hotel for the Disney Marathon will be Disney's Port Orleans Resort. JDRF covers hotel accommodations (double occupancy) based on the fundraising package selected. For more information on the fundraising please click here. Please note, private rooms are accommodated based on availability, and for an additional fee.

Fundraising

1. Raise the Funds for Research

Team JDRF to Cure Diabetes is a partnership: Runners commit to raising a minimum dollar amount and in exchange, JDRF provides each runner with support and coaching in the months leading up to and throughout the Race weekend. Upon registering, you are asked to select your level of fundraising (click here for the fundraising options) - packages start at $2,000. JDRF has created powerful online fundraising tools to make it easy for you to reach your goal. Your local JDRF chapter will help you throughout your process with materials, ideas, and support.

2. Age Requirements

Full marathon runners must be 18 years of age or older by the date of the Race. Half marathon runners must be 14 years of age or older by the date of the Race and must have a signed parental/guardian consent form.

3. Complete the Registration Form

All runners must fully complete the registration form and submit a non-refundable registration fee. The registration fee must be paid during the registration process via credit card and will count towards your fundraising minimum. Click here to access the registration form online.

4. Agree to the Contract and Financial Policies & Commitments

Upon completing your registration, you will be asked to commit to being responsible for the remaining balance of your fundraising minimum, should you not reach it. We make a commitment to provide you with support for your Race weekend and the training to get you prepared for it. It is important to the program that you are able to commit to reaching your fundraising minimum and we are here to help you every step of the way.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Team JDRF?

Team JDRF to Cure Diabetes is a unique and exciting way to fight diabetes. Participants choose Run locations and run for their choice of distance (usually a half or a full marathon) to raise money for JDRF. Runners from around the country take on two challenges: raising the money needed to fund research for better treatments and a cure for diabetes, and reaching a personal training goal to prepare for a race experience.

Where is my local chapter?

Working together, JDRF has more than 100 Chapters, Branches, and Affiliates worldwide. Please click here to locate your local JDRF chapter or call 888.533.9255 for further information.

Who can run?

Full marathon runners must be 18 years of age or older by the date of the Race. lf marathon runners must be 14 years of age or older by the date of the Race and must have a signed parental/guardian consent form.

Is there a deadline for meeting my fundraising minimum?

Yes. We understand that this may be the most money you have ever attempted to raise and you may feel unsure about whether you will be able to do it. We have found that if you follow the recommended guidelines and use the materials provided, you will be more successful than you think! Contact your JDRF Chapter for detailed information on fundraising schedules and timelines developed to help you reach your goals.

Isn't it hard to meet the fundraising requirement?

Not according to many of our past participants. Once you start contacting people, you will be surprised how quickly the money (and the admiration) rolls in. JDRF has created powerful online fundraising tools to make it easier for you to reach your goal, and your local JDRF chapter can help you with materials, ideas, and support.

Are there different fundraising packages available?

Yes, as a runner, you'll commit to a fundraising minimum (packages start at $2,000) and in exchange, JDRF will provide you with support and coaching during the months leading up to as well as throughout the JDRF Ride weekend. JDRF has created powerful online fundraising tools to make it easier for you to reach your goal, and your local JDRF chapter can help you with materials, ideas, and support.

Does the required registration fee count towards my fundraising?

Yes! The required registration fee will count toward your fundraising.

What kind of training should I do?

Our National Coach, Josh Maio, has excellent training plans for preparing for endurance Runs. We will help you adopt a training schedule and plan that is right for you.

How much time do I have to commit to this?

How much time you train is completely up to you. Your JDRF Coach can help you determine the best time commitment for your fitness level. If you are a novice, most coaches will recommend you begin training 16 weeks before a full marathon and 12 weeks before a half marathon.
