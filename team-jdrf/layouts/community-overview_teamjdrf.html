How it Works

Creating your event is as easy as 1, 2, 3! Follow three simple steps to host a community event.

1. Select an activity that appeals to you

What do you like to do? Find an activity you enjoy sharing with others. Your idea might be the next big thing in T1D fundraising!

2. Set the date

Pick a time that will attract as many participants or customers as possible. Consider the time of year, target audience, and other factors that will boost your success.

3. Register Online

In the "Get Involved" section choose your type of event. Then tell us about your event and begin using our fantastic online tools to promote it, recruit participants, and fundraise right away.
